Be the Voice of Change in Your Community

Report crimes, corruption, and environmental violations with evidence. Your voice matters in building a transparent and accountable society.

Key Features

Aadhaar Verification

Secure registration using Aadhaar ensures authenticity and prevents fake reports, maintaining the integrity of the platform.

One Aadhaar, One Vote

Our unique voting system ensures fair prioritization of reports through democratic community action.

Multimedia Evidence

Upload photos, videos, or audio recordings as evidence to strengthen your reports and increase credibility.

Local Notifications

Get instant notifications about reports in your area, fostering community awareness and collective action.

Judicial Integration

Reports with high community support are forwarded to judicial authorities for attention and action.

Geo-tagged Reports

Precise location tagging helps authorities respond effectively to the reported incidents.

How It Works

1

Register with Aadhaar

Complete a secure one-time registration process using your Aadhaar number for identity verification.

2

Document the Issue

Capture evidence of the violation or issue using your phone's camera or audio recorder.

3

Submit Your Report

Create a detailed report with your evidence, location, and description of the incident.

4

Community Voting

Local users vote on reports to prioritize serious issues and filter out less critical ones.

5

Action and Resolution

Action and Resolution

High-priority reports are forwarded to relevant authorities for investigation and appropriate action.
